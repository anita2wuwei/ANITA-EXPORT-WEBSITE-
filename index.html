About WW Export

Founded in 2021, WW Export was established after years of hands-on involvement in global manufacturing and international trade. Before forming our own company, our team had already served hundreds of enterprises across electronics, consumer products, and industrial supply chains.

We specialize in cleaning appliances, smart short-distance mobility, and energy storage products, providing OEM / ODM solutions tailored to global markets.

What differentiates us is not scale, but depth of experience. Our core members have spent decades inside real enterprises — growing with them from early stages through expansion, system building, and globalization.
